Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is link routing?
    Link routing is the process of directing user transitions based on various conditions such as OS, browser, country, and more. It allows you to optimize traffic distribution for better results.
  • How does spare links feature work?
    Spare links serve as backup redirections in case the primary links get blocked by different services. It ensures a seamless user experience even in the face of potential link restrictions.
  • What is link monitoring and domains?
    All domains and links are constantly monitored for blocking by various services such as Google Safe Browsing, AdBlock, PhishFort, Ultimate Hosts Blacklist, and Metamask. Users receive notifications if any blocking occurs.
  • How many domains can I use?
    You can use more than 100 domains for your links. Additionally, you have the option to link your own domains and generate SSL certificates.
  • Can I track detailed statistics?
    Yes, you can track statistics by days, countries, cities, devices, brands of mobile devices, OS, browsers, languages, Internet providers, and used proxies. Detailed information is available for each transition, starting from the source.
  • What is cloaking?
    Cloaking allows you to hide the main site from the eyes of advertising network moderators or extra users. You can specify a "white" site that users will see when clicking on a link, with or without a redirect.
  • How does the Teams feature work?
    The Teams feature allows you to add users, assign roles, and provide access to the system. You can connect Telegram groups for teams to receive important notifications and promptly react to changes.
  • Is the server abuse-proof?
    Yes, our abuse-proof server ensures that your links remain intact and safe. Unlike other link managers, we prioritize protecting your links and valuable traffic.
  • How do macros work?
    Macros allow you to insert information about the user's device, as well as details about their country, city, mobile operator, and more, into the link.
  • Is an API available?
    Yes, we provide an API that allows you to track detailed statistics on clicks, transitions, and user information. It offers flexibility and integration capabilities for advanced users.
